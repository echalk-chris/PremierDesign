Alief Independent School District

  • Martin Elementary staff members recently marked the end of the summer by participating in activities at the Alief YMCA, a community business partner of the school district. Read more...
  • Alief ISD is off to a great start for the 2013-2014 school year! Please stay tuned for more photos from across the district.
  • Five Alief Floral students were recently recognized at the 99th Texas State Floral Convention for their achievement in Floral Design. Congratulations to Elizabeth P. (Elsik), Rosa A. (Elsik), Berenice B. (Elsik), Sagrario T. (Hastings) and Donna M. (Elsik).
  • About 40 Alief ISD graduates returned to give back to the district by choosing Alief as their place of employment. They gathered for a photo during the New Teacher Celebration portion of Alief U.
  • Three Alief ISD student-athletes were selected to the all Greater Houston Preseason UIL High School Football team.  Elsik’s Ishmael Z. along with Taylor’s Keenen B. and Chris H. were honored by the Touchdown Club of Houston during the annual Touchdowner of the Year banquet. Only 29 players from the area were honored. Each player received a plaque from Bob McNair, owner of the Houston Texans.
  • Holmquist Elementary won third place in the national Big Lots Lots2Give video contest. The school received $3,433.34, which includes the $2,000 prize from the contest and a percentage of in-store donations.

Interested in making a difference?

Join the Alief ISD Educational Improvement Council!

     The Alief ISD Educational Improvement Council (EIC) is a district-level committee that serves in an advisory role to the superintendent.
     During the school year, the committee meets monthly to learn about and provide input on a variety of district programs and practices.
     The EIC is comprised of elected campus teachers and other professionals, as well as other district staff, volunteer parents and students, and business and community members.
     If you are interested in serving on the EIC, please contact Karla Kessler at 281-498-8110, ext. 4900.
